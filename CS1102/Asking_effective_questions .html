Question Formation Process

Primary Question:

"What variables affect car prices?"

Development Process:

  • Examined dataset structure
  • Identified measurable characteristics
  • Considered market dynamics
  • Looked for quantifiable relationships

Supporting Questions

  • How strong is the correlation between technical specs and price?
  • Do physical dimensions significantly impact pricing?
  • What is the relationship between fuel efficiency and price?
  • Are there any unexpected relationships in the data?

Question Refinement Journey

  1. Initial broad question:

    "What determines car prices?"

  2. Narrowing process based on:
    • Available data points
    • Measurable characteristics
    • Industry knowledge
  3. Final focused question:

    "Which specific variables have the strongest correlation with car prices?"
